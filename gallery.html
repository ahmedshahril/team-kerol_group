Art Gallery

Bank Negara Malaysia’s Art Collection

Gallery Info

The Art Gallery is a rotating exhibition of the Central Bank of Malaysia's art collection. There are paintings, prints, drawings and sculptures
reflecting the progress of the nation and highlighting the support of art by the Central Bank, including works by early masters such as
Mohd Hoessein Enas, Yong Mun Sen, Chuah Thean Teng, Syed Ahmad Jamal, Ibrahim Hussein up to the present day of young artist.


Location

3rd Floor


Operating Hour

Open daily: 10.00am - 6.00pm
