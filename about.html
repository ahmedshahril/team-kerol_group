About

Bank Negara Malaysia Museum and Art Gallery (BNM MAG)

Through its six permanent galleries, the Bank Negara Malaysia Museum and Art Gallery offers a number of themes.

  • The Children's Gallery
  • he Bank Negara Malaysia Gallery
  • The Economics Gallery
  • The Islamic Finance Gallery
  • The Numismatics Gallery
  • The Art Gallery

These galleries delve into the Malaysian economy, the position of the Bank since pre-independence to the present day in the economic growth and financial landscape of the country. The Museum also presents numismatic treasures as old as history, as well as its dynamic collection of Malaysian and ASEAN artworks, reinforcing the role of the Bank as the leader of national numismatic and supporter of art heritage.




Bank Negara Malaysia

Founded on 26 January 1959 under the Act 1958 of the Central Bank of Malaya (CBA 1958). The CBA 1958 was surpassed by the Act 2009 of the Central Bank of Malaysia, which became effective on 25 November 2009. It is a statutory company owned wholly by the Government of Malaysia. Bank Negara Malaysia serves as the government's banker and financial adviser.

The monetary policy stance of Bank Negara Malaysia is to maintain price stability while staying supportive of development. Financial system stability is also the responsibility of Bank Negara Malaysia. This is done through the growth of a financial sector that is sound, resilient, inclusive and diversified and helps to support the sectors of the real economy. It also plays an important role in the implementation of deepening and strengthening policies in the financial markets, including the foreign exchange market.
